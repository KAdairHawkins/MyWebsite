About Adair

Education and Experience

After receiving her bachelors in English, Adair traveled to the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex to get her masters in education from Dallas Baptist University. She always loved writing stories and began teaching herself how to "speak computer" when she started doing background research on a character for a novel she wanted to write. Now she has two passions, writing and coding.

Since then, she has created over a dozen programs and websites. She obtained a graduate certificate in full stack web development from Southern Methodist University.
